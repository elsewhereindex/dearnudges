Dear: Stay in Touch

A quiet nudge for the people on your mind.

Dear sends you one gentle nudge a day reminding you to reach out to someone you care about. Your nudge might come from Sherlock Holmes, a haiku poet, or a French fox named Le Renard.

Research shows people consistently underestimate how much others appreciate hearing from them -- especially after a long silence. Dear closes that gap, one nudge at a time.
