Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2026

Dear does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no tracking. We don't know who you are, who your friends are, or how you use the app.

That's it. That's the privacy policy.

If you want the longer version for legal purposes, keep reading.

What data Dear accesses

Contact names and photos. During onboarding, Dear asks for permission to access your Contacts. This is used solely to let you select people to add to your nudge list. Contact data is read once during selection and stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Dear never transmits contact data off your device, and you can use the app without granting Contacts access by typing names manually.

Notification permissions. Dear requests permission to send local notifications so it can remind you to reach out to someone. These notifications are scheduled entirely on-device. No server is involved.

What data Dear stores

All data is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework and UserDefaults (via App Group for widget functionality). This includes:

  • The names and optional photos of people you've added
  • Your nudge preferences (frequency, notification time)
  • Your persona and theme selections
  • Nudge completion history
  • Purchase status for subscriptions and voice personas

This data never leaves your device. There is no cloud sync, no backup to our servers, and no way for us to access it.

What data Dear collects or transmits

None. Zero. Dear has no server, no backend, no API, no database, and no analytics. We do not use any third-party SDKs, tracking pixels, advertising frameworks, or analytics tools. We do not collect crash reports. We do not know how many people use the app beyond what Apple provides in aggregate App Store analytics (which contains no personally identifiable information and is controlled by your device settings).

In-app purchases

Dear offers optional subscriptions and one-time purchases through Apple's StoreKit framework. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not process, store, or have access to any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment data.

Third-party services

Dear uses no third-party services. The app operates entirely offline using Apple-native frameworks. StoreKit (for in-app purchases) communicates with Apple's servers, not ours.

Data sharing

We do not share data with anyone because we do not have any data to share.

Children's privacy

Dear does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no information from anyone, this is inherently satisfied.

Dear Friend

If you choose to become a Dear Friend (a patron tier within Dear), you may optionally share information with the developer through email. This is entirely voluntary and happens outside the app.

The Dear Friend post-purchase screen may help you compose an email to the developer. This email may include:

  • Your preferred name, if you choose to be listed in Dear's credits
  • Your mailing address, if you are a Founding Dear Friend and would like to receive a handwritten note

This email is composed on your device using your own email app. You review and send it yourself. Dear does not collect, store, or transmit this information through any server or API. The developer receives it only through your email and uses it only for the stated purposes.

Your email address is visible to the developer when you send the message. It will not be added to any marketing list or shared with anyone else.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Dear handles data (for example, adding optional cloud sync in a future version), we will update this policy and note the changes here. Any future feature that involves data transmission will be opt-in and clearly disclosed.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at support@dearnudges.com.
